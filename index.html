About Our Technology

Learn about the advanced machine learning behind our cardiovascular assessment tool

Advanced Machine Learning for Health

Our cardiovascular assessment model is built using XGBoost, a powerful machine learning algorithm trained on comprehensive health datasets. The model analyzes multiple health parameters to identify patterns associated with cardiovascular conditions.

By considering factors such as blood pressure, cholesterol levels, glucose levels, BMI, smoking status, age, and gender, our AI provides personalized risk assessments that can help individuals make informed decisions about their heart health.

While our tool provides valuable insights, it's important to note that it doesn't replace professional medical advice. Always consult with healthcare providers for comprehensive health assessments.
